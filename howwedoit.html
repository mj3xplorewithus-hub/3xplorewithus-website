The Choice

There's the choice to do what everyone else does and dream of something else.
Or there's doing it.

You need a plan and a goal. It took us years to get where we are now — but we did it, and we couldn't be happier.

Yes, you can always wait for more money, more backups, and more safety. But you get old like that.

If it doesn't work out, we'll sell the truck and start again. But when you truly commit, you learn a lot about yourself. There are always answers and possibilities — you just have to be realistic and willing to see them.

You can always go back to a normal job.
But we're trying something else.

A Different Life

It's a different way of living.

It comes with pros and cons, sacrifices and real freedoms most people never get to experience. This is what our daily life actually looks like — the choices we've made and why we wouldn't trade it.

We live humbly.

Not because we have to, but because we choose to. Simplicity creates space for what truly matters.

We always cook.

Restaurants are rare. The food we make is healthier, tastes better, and we know exactly what goes into it.

We rarely go shopping.

Unless it's for the truck or our travels. We happily stepped out of the endless consumerism cycle.

We don't have fancy clothes.

No brands, no status symbols. We have a truck and freedom instead — to us that's a no-brainer.

Our idea of fun is freedom.

Spending time together. Going on hikes, taking photos, getting creative, exploring the world and talking to people. This is life. This is freedom.

This is our kind of success.

Having time and freedom. We live with each other 24/7 and we love it. We've built the life we live — and it couldn't be better.

Strip away the noise.

Strip away social media, endless consumerism and capitalism — and you realise you don't need much. Most products are just fancy packaging and marketing. What you actually need are simple, low-cost products that do the job without the chemicals and nonsense.

Full control of our budget.

We track everything. We have full overview and control of our money at all times. That clarity is part of the freedom.
