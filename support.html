Why It Matters

This channel runs differently.

No sponsorships. No brand deals. No algorithm-chasing. We made a conscious decision when we started this: we wouldn't compromise what we make to pay for it.

That means 3xploreWithUs is funded entirely by people who watch it. No hidden agenda. No product placements sneaked into the edit. No awkward mid-roll reads for a VPN we don't use. Just honest filmmaking about a life we actually believe in.

We left behind careers, mortgages and the script most people follow. We built a truck from scratch, put a baby in it, and pointed it at the horizon. We're not going to sell that out for a sponsored post.

If you've ever watched one of our videos and felt something — curiosity, longing, a quiet reminder that there's another way — then this page is for you.
