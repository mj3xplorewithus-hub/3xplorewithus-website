Laundry Detergent
The recipe

How we make it

Ingredients
  • 100 g Grated natural bar soapOne full 100 g bar, grated as finely as you can.
  • 200 g Baking sodaSodium bicarbonate.
  • 200 g Washing sodaSodium carbonate — not the same as baking soda.
  • 100 g Epsom salt (optional)For extra water softening in hard-water regions.
  • Few drops Essential oil (optional)Omit entirely for baby clothes.
Method
  1. Grate the soap bar finely. A box grater works; a food processor is faster.
  2. Mix thoroughly with the baking soda, washing soda, and Epsom salt if using.
  3. Add essential oil last, drop by drop, stirring as you go. Skip for baby loads.
  4. Store in a glass jar or airtight container. Keep dry.
Usage

1–2 tablespoons per load. Adjust for machine size, water hardness, and how dirty the wash is. Add 1/2 cup white vinegar to the fabric softener compartment for extra softness and freshness.

Heads up

Washing soda and baking soda are not interchangeable. If your local shop only stocks baking soda, you can convert it: bake at 200°C for an hour on a tray, stirring once. The texture changes from fine to gritty — that's washing soda.

A note
