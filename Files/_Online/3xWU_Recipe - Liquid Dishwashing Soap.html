Liquid Dishwashing Soap
The recipe

How we make it

Ingredients
  • 40–60 g Grated natural bar soapAbout half a 100 g bar.
  • 500 ml Warm distilled waterPlus ~100 ml extra hot water for step 1.
  • 1–2 tsp Washing sodaDissolve first in a little hot water (see method).
  • 1–2 tsp Vegetable glycerin (optional)Adds mildness and viscosity.
  • Pinch Citric acid or lemon juice (optional)Add just before use — do not store in the bottle.
Method
  1. Dissolve the washing soda in approx. 100 ml hot water. Set aside.
  2. Combine the rest. Add the remaining water and grated soap. Stir gently until fully dissolved — 5 to 10 minutes.
  3. Stir in the glycerin (if using). Let cool completely.
  4. Bottle it. Pour into a squeeze bottle. Shake well before each use.
Usage

1–2 squirts per sink of hot water. For very greasy pans, sprinkle extra baking soda directly on the pan and scrub.

Why 40–60 g?

Effective but not overly thick or drying. Adjust the water up or down to thin or thicken the consistency to your liking.
