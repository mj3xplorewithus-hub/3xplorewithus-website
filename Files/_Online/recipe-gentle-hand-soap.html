Gentle Hand Soap
The recipe

How we make it

Ingredients
  • 20–30 g Grated natural bar soapAbout a quarter of a 100 g bar.
  • 250 ml Distilled waterFiltered or boiled-and-cooled works in a pinch.
  • 1 tsp Vegetable glycerin~5 ml. Adds slip and stops the soap drying out hands.
Foaming dispenser?

Fill the bottle 1/4 with the soap mix, top up with water. The foamer does the rest — uses less per pump and lasts longer.

Method
  1. Heat the water. Just-boiled is ideal — you want the soap to dissolve quickly.
  2. Add the grated soap. Stir gently until fully dissolved. No lumps, no streaks.
  3. Stir in the glycerin. Let the mixture cool completely before bottling — it thickens as it cools.
  4. Pour into a pump bottle. Shake before first use. Done.
Storage

No preservatives, so make small batches and use within 3–4 weeks. Smells off? Bin it and start fresh — you'll only lose 30 cents.
